<p><p>The industrial ultrasonic cleaners market is primarily categorized into single tank type and multi-tank type systems. Single tank types consist of one chamber that facilitates the cleaning of items in one cycle, making them suitable for smaller operations or specific tasks. In contrast, multi-tank types feature multiple chambers, allowing for sequential cleaning processes, such as washing, rinsing, and drying. This versatility enhances efficiency and productivity, making multi-tank systems ideal for larger industries requiring varied cleaning stages.</p></p>
