<p><ul><li>Lifting And Traversing Class</li><li>Simple Lift</li><li>Roadway Stacking</li><li>Plane Movement</li><li>Vertical Lift</li><li>Vertical Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Three-dimensional parking lots can be categorized into various market types based on their mechanism. Lifting and traversing systems utilize platforms that lift and move vehicles horizontally. Simple lifts raise vehicles vertically for compact storage. Roadway stacking organizes cars in a linear fashion for efficient space use. Plane movement systems allow cars to maneuver on a two-dimensional plane, while vertical lifts directly elevate vehicles. Vertical loop systems feature a circular movement design for efficient access and compact storage solutions.</p></p>
