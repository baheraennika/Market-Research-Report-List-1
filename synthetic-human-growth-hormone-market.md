<p><p>The synthetic human growth hormone (HGH) market can be categorized into two main types: powder and solvent forms. Powdered HGH is a dry formulation that requires reconstitution with a diluent before administration, often favored for its stability and longer shelf life. In contrast, solvent-based HGH is already in liquid form, allowing for immediate use, which can enhance convenience for patients. Both forms are used in various medical applications, including hormone deficiency treatment and anti-aging therapies.</p></p>
