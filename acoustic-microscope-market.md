<p><ul><li>Scanning Acoustic Microscope (SAM)</li><li>Confocal Scanning Acoustic Microscope (CSAM)</li><li>C-Mode Scanning Acoustic Microscope</li><li>Scanning Laser Acoustic Microscope (SLAM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic microscope market includes various technologies catering to diverse applications. Scanning Acoustic Microscopes (SAM) use high-frequency sound waves for imaging, offering detailed insights into material structures. Confocal Scanning Acoustic Microscopes (CSAM) enhance resolution by integrating confocal techniques, allowing for improved 3D imaging. C-Mode Scanning Acoustic Microscopes provide insights into specimen layers, useful for subsurface evaluations. Scanning Laser Acoustic Microscopes (SLAM) combine laser technology with acoustic principles, enabling precise imaging and analysis of materials, particularly in biomedical fields.</p></p>
