<p><p>The water-based nano ceramic coating market is divided into two main types: powder and liquid. Powder coatings are formulated as dry particles that, when mixed with a liquid medium, form a durable, high-performance protective layer, offering superior hardness and chemical resistance. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, are ready-to-apply formulations that provide excellent adhesion and flexibility. Both types are favored for their eco-friendliness and minimal volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions, making them suitable for various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
