<p><ul><li>Conventional Glaucoma Surgeries</li><li>Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgeries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glaucoma market is categorized into two main types: Conventional Glaucoma Surgeries and Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgeries (MIGS). Conventional surgeries include procedures like trabeculectomy and tube shunt surgeries, which involve more extensive tissue manipulation and carry higher risks. MIGS, on the other hand, are designed to lower intraocular pressure with less trauma, offering faster recovery and fewer complications. Both types aim to manage glaucoma effectively, catering to varying patient needs and preferences based on disease severity and treatment goals.</p></p>
