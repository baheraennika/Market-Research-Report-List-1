<p><p>The SWIR imaging cameras market is primarily divided into two types: SWIR area scan cameras and SWIR line scan cameras. SWIR area scan cameras capture images in a two-dimensional format, ideal for applications requiring detailed imaging of static scenes or objects. In contrast, SWIR line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them suitable for high-speed applications such as conveyor belt inspection. Both types leverage shortwave infrared technology for enhanced imaging in various industrial and research sectors.</p></p>
