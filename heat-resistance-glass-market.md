<p><p>The heat resistance glass market comprises three main types: tempered glass, Pyrex glass, and Pyroceram glass. Tempered glass, known for its strength and thermal stability, is widely used in building facades and automotive applications. Pyrex glass, made from borosilicate, is favored in kitchenware and laboratory equipment due to its ability to withstand thermal shock. Pyroceram glass, with its superior thermal resistance, is employed in cooktops and ovenware, making it ideal for high-temperature applications. Each type serves distinct needs in various industries.</p></p>
