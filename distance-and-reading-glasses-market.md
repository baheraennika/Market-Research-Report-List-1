<p><p>The distance and reading glasses market encompasses various lens types tailored to different vision needs. Single vision lenses correct one field of vision, either near or far. Bifocal lenses have distinct sections for near and distance vision. Trifocal lenses offer three viewing areas—near, intermediate, and distance. Progressive lenses provide a seamless transition between multiple vision zones without visible lines, catering to presbyopia. Each lens type addresses specific visual challenges, making them essential for users seeking clarity at various distances.</p></p>
