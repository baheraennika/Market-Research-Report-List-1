<p><p>Incline conveyors are categorized into two primary market types: flat belt conveyors and modular belt conveyors. Flat belt conveyors utilize a continuous belt stretched over pulleys, designed for smooth transportation of materials at various angles. They are ideal for bulk items due to their simplicity. Alternatively, modular belt conveyors consist of interlocking plastic segments, enabling flexibility and easy maintenance. These are suitable for handling small and irregularly shaped items, making them versatile for different industries.</p></p>
