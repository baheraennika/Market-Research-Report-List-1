<p><p>Dental practice software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software operates over the internet, allowing dental practices to access their systems from any device with internet connectivity. This model typically offers greater flexibility, automatic updates, and reduced IT management costs. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on a practice's servers, providing full control over data and functionality but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both options serve to enhance practice efficiency and patient care.</p></p>
