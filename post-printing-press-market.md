<p><p>In the post-printing press market, there are primarily two types: automatic and semi-automatic systems. Automatic machines operate with minimal human intervention, offering high efficiency and fast production rates, making them ideal for large-scale operations. Conversely, semi-automatic systems require some manual input, providing greater flexibility and lower initial investment, suited for smaller businesses or varied production runs. Both types cater to different operational needs, balancing the trade-off between speed, labor costs, and production versatility in the printing industry.</p></p>
