<p><strong>The Industrial Storage Tank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized</li><li>Non-Pressurized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial storage tank market is primarily categorized into pressurized and non-pressurized tanks. Pressurized tanks are designed to hold liquids or gases under pressure, allowing for the safe storage of volatile substances and preventing vapor release. These tanks are commonly used in industries like petrochemicals and natural gas. Non-pressurized tanks, on the other hand, store fluids at ambient pressure, suitable for a wide range of applications, including water and chemical storage. Their design prioritizes ease of access and lower construction costs.</p></p>
