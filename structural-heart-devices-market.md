<p><p>The Structural Heart Devices market encompasses various interventions for heart conditions. Surgical Aortic Valve Replacement (SAVR) involves open-heart surgery to replace damaged aortic valves. Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR) is a less invasive technique utilizing catheter-based methods for valve replacement. Mitral Repair addresses mitral valve issues through either surgical or catheter-based solutions. Left Atrial Appendage Closure (LAAC) aims to reduce stroke risk in atrial fibrillation patients by sealing off the left atrial appendage, preventing blood clot formation.</p></p>
