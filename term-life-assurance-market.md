<p><p>Term Life Assurance offers different market types, including Renewable Term Life Insurance and Return of Premium Term Life Insurance. Renewable Term Life allows policyholders to renew their coverage at the end of the term without medical underwriting, providing flexibility as needs change. In contrast, Return of Premium Term Life Insurance refunds all premiums paid if the insured survives the term, offering savings potential. Both options cater to varied financial goals, balancing affordability with long-term security and investment returns.</p></p>
