<p><p>The optical chemical sensors market comprises several types, including infrared sensors, which detect gases based on their molecular absorption characteristics; fiber optic sensors, which utilize light transmission through fiber optic cables to monitor chemical concentrations; and photoionization sensors, which employ ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules for detection. Other sensor types include fluorescence-based and electrochemical sensors. Together, these technologies enable real-time monitoring and analysis of chemical substances, enhancing applications in environmental monitoring, industrial processes, and safety systems.</p></p>
