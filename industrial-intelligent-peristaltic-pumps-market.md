<p><p>The Industrial Intelligent Peristaltic Pumps market comprises two key types: Peristaltic Tube Pumps and Peristaltic Hose Pumps. Peristaltic Tube Pumps utilize flexible tubes to transport fluids, providing precise flow control and low shear rates, making them ideal for sensitive applications. In contrast, Peristaltic Hose Pumps use a resilient hose to accommodate higher pressures and viscous fluids, ensuring durability and reliability in challenging environments. Both types offer advantages in efficiency, ease of maintenance, and contamination prevention, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
