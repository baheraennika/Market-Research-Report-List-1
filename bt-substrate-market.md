<p><p>BT Substrate market encompasses different types, including Wafer-Level Ball Grid Array (WB BGA), Wafer-Level Chip Scale Package (WB CSP), and Fan-Out Chip Scale Package (FC CSP). WB BGA is known for its efficiency in packaging and thermal performance, while WB CSP provides compact, high-density solutions suitable for advanced mobile applications. FC CSP, on the other hand, allows for better performance with its embedded die technology, making it ideal for high-speed and high-frequency devices. These variations cater to distinct application needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
