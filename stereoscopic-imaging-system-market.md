<p><ul><li>Lens Stereoscope</li><li>Mirror Stereoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stereoscopic imaging system market consists of various types, primarily lens and mirror stereoscopes. Lens stereoscopes utilize dual lenses to create a three-dimensional effect by displaying two images side by side, enhancing depth perception. In contrast, mirror stereoscopes employ mirrors to reflect images into each eye, providing a similar 3D experience. Both types are used in diverse applications, including photography, virtual reality, and educational tools, catering to users seeking immersive visual experiences and greater depth in imagery.</p></p>
