<p><p>The antiulcerant drugs market encompasses several key types. Proton pump inhibitors reduce stomach acid production, providing potent relief from ulcers. H2 receptor antagonists also lower acid secretion but have a different mechanism of action. Gastric mucosal protective drugs enhance the stomach's defenses against acid damage. Antibiotics target Helocobacter pylori infections linked to ulcers. Antacids neutralize existing stomach acid for quick relief, while prostaglandin agonists help protect the gastric lining, enhancing mucosal defense and promoting healing.</p></p>
