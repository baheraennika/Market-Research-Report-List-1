<p><p>Safety shut-off valves for gas meters are critical components designed to prevent gas leaks and ensure safety in gas distribution systems. Motorised valves utilize electric motors to open or close the valve, allowing for remote operation and automation. They are ideal for systems requiring precise control. Solenoid valves, on the other hand, use electromagnetic coils to actuate the valve, providing quick response times and reliability. Both types enhance safety measures by promptly stopping gas flow in emergency situations.</p></p>
