<p><p>Color filters for displays enhance visual quality by manipulating light wavelengths. RGB primary color filters, consisting of red, green, and blue, are widely used in screens to create vibrant images by combining these three colors. Conversely, CMYG complementary color filters, incorporating cyan, magenta, yellow, and black, focus on reducing light interference and improving color accuracy in printing and display technologies. Both types play crucial roles in achieving precise color reproduction and enhancing the overall viewing experience in digital devices.</p></p>
