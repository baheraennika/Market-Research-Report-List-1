<p><p>The motorcycle electronic immobilizer market is divided into two main types: built-in type immobilizers and external type immobilizers. Built-in type immobilizers are integrated directly into the motorcycle's electrical system, providing seamless security and preventing unauthorized ignition. External type immobilizers, on the other hand, are standalone devices that can be added to any motorcycle, offering flexibility and additional security features. Both types enhance vehicle safety by deterring theft and unauthorized use, catering to different consumer preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
