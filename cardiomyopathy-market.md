<p><p>The cardiomyopathy market is categorized into three main types. Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy, marked by abnormal thickening of the heart muscle, presents unique management challenges and a targeted therapeutic market. Restrictive cardiomyopathy, where the heart becomes rigid and less able to fill with blood, represents a niche segment in the market, requiring specialized therapeutic approaches and patient management strategies.</p></p>
