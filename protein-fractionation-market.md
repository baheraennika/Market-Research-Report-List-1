<p><p>The protein fractionation market encompasses various types, including Immunoglobulin Secretion (IS), FLAG-tag, GST-tag, anion-exchange, cation-exchange, DNA-binding proteins, and maltose-binding proteins. IS focuses on isolating antibodies, while FLAG and GST tags facilitate protein purification through affinity methods. Anion and cation exchange methods separate proteins based on charge. DNA-binding proteins target specific interactions with nucleic acids, while maltose-binding proteins are used for affinity purification. Each type serves distinct applications in research, biotechnology, and pharmaceuticals, enhancing protein study and production.</p></p>
