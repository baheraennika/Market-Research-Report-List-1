<p><p>The N95 protective mask filter material market primarily consists of two main types: PP (polypropylene) nonwoven and PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) nonwoven materials. PP nonwoven is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and efficient filtration capabilities, making it a standard choice for N95 masks. In contrast, PTFE nonwoven material offers superior breathability and chemical resistance, enhancing comfort and performance. Both types are essential for meeting the filtration standards required in high-quality respiratory protection against airborne particles.</p></p>
