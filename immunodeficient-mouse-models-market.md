<p><p>The immunodeficient mouse models market is segmented into three main types. Severe Immunodeficient Models, including NOD/SCID and NSG mice, exhibit further immune deficits, enabling the engraftment of human cells for advanced studies. Next-generation Severe Immunodeficient Models offer enhanced characteristics and customization, fostering innovative research in immunology, regenerative medicine, and drug development.</p></p>
