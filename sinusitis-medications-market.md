<p><p>The sinusitis medications market comprises three primary types: antibiotics, corticosteroids, and decongestants. Antibiotics are used to treat bacterial infections that can cause sinusitis, effectively reducing symptoms and speeding recovery. Corticosteroids help to reduce inflammation in the nasal passages, alleviating congestion and discomfort. Decongestants work by narrowing blood vessels in the nasal area, leading to reduced swelling and improved airflow. Together, these medication types address the various symptoms and underlying causes of sinusitis, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
