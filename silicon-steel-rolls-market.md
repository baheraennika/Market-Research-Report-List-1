<p><strong>The Silicon Steel Rolls Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel</li><li>Non-Grain Oriented Electrical Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Steel Rolls market is divided into two main types: oriented electrical steel and non-grain oriented electrical steel. Oriented electrical steel is designed for applications requiring high magnetic performance, primarily in transformer cores and electric motors. It is characterized by grain alignment, which enhances its magnetic properties. Conversely, non-grain oriented electrical steel has a uniform microstructure, making it suitable for various applications like generators and appliances, where isotropic magnetic properties are advantageous. Both types are essential for improving energy efficiency in electrical devices.</p></p>
