<p><strong>The SUV and Pickup Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SUV and Pickup Wheel Speed Sensor market includes two main types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed readings crucial for anti-lock braking systems and traction control. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors rely on changes in magnetic fields generated by rotating components to produce an electrical signal indicative of wheel speed. Both types play a vital role in enhancing vehicle safety and performance, catering to the specific needs of SUVs and pickups.</p></p>
