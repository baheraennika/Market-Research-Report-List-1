<p><p>The grease recovery device (GRD) market is segmented into two main types: installed underground and freestanding. Installed underground devices are integrated into the plumbing system, offering a discreet solution that minimizes space usage while efficiently capturing grease and food particles before they enter the sewer system. Freestanding units, on the other hand, are positioned above ground and are typically more accessible for servicing and maintenance. Both types are essential for maintaining proper waste management in commercial kitchens and food service establishments.</p></p>
