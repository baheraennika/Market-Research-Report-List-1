<p><p>The Vehicle Lidar Optical Components Market is categorized into two primary types: receiving end and transmitting end. In contrast, the transmitting end focuses on components that emit laser pulses, including lasers and beam shaping optics. These components work together to create a comprehensive sensing system, essential for enabling advanced driver-assistance systems and autonomous vehicle navigation.</p></p>
