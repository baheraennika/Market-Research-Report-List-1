<p><p>The adhesives for optoelectronics market is categorized into three types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives offer versatility and ease of application, making them suitable for bonding delicate components like sensors and displays. Solid adhesives provide strong, durable bonds and are ideal for high-performance applications where rigidity is essential. Paste adhesives, typically used in the assembly of electronic circuits, offer excellent gap-filling properties and control during application. Each type caters to specific bonding needs in optoelectronic devices.</p></p>
