<p><ul><li>"Water-Cooling Strand Pelletizing System"</li><li>"Air-Cooling Strand Pelletizing System"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Strand Palletizing System market features two primary types: Water-Cooling and Air-Cooling systems. Water-Cooling Strand Pelletizing Systems utilize water to quickly cool and solidify extruded polymer strands, ensuring efficient processing and high-quality pellets. Conversely, Air-Cooling Strand Pelletizing Systems rely on ambient air to cool the strands, offering advantages like energy efficiency and reduced water usage. Both systems cater to different production needs in the plastic manufacturing industry, influencing factors such as cost, efficiency, and environmental impact.</p></p>
