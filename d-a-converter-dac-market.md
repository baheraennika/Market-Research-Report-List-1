<p><p>Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs) are essential in converting digital signals into analog signals for various applications. The R-2R DAC uses a resistor ladder network for precise conversion, offering simplicity and low cost. High-Speed Current-Steering DACs, on the other hand, excel in speed and are used in high-frequency applications due to their fast switching capabilities. Other market types may include Sigma-Delta DACs, which focus on high resolution, and PWM DACs, which utilize pulse-width modulation for signal generation, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
